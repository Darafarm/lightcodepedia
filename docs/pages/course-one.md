# HCI - 1
## Foundations and User-Centric Design

This introductory course establishes the core philosophies of Human-Computer Interaction. 
Students will explore cognitive psychology principles, user interface guidelines, and the fundamental heuristics of usability. 
The curriculum focuses on the early stages of the design lifecycle, teaching students 
how to conduct effective user research, gather requirements, and translate insights into low-fidelity wireframes. 
By understanding how humans perceive and process digital information, participants learn to advocate 
for the user and design intuitive, accessible interfaces from the ground up.
